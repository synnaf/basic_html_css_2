Who is Heléne?

Heléne is a silver and jewellry designer based in Gävle, Sweden. All the jewellry is made of silver and is designed and made in her home. The designs are available in a limited capacity - 5 of each piece only. Contact us if you have any questions about the work or if you are interested in a custom design!
